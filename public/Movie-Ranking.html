The Best Movies According to Brandon

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

The Other Guys

This is one of the funniest movies I have ever watched.
The combination of Will Ferrel and Mark Whalberg is something special.
Most movies similar to this try too hard to be funny, whereas this movie just
does a great job of being serious when it needs too and dropping jokes at the right time.

The Dark Knight

The most iconic Batman movie ever made, starring Christian Bale as Batman,
and Heath Ledger's legendary showcase of the Clown Prince of Crime Joker.
As an avid Batman comic reader, this movie was so surreal and just one of those
movies that I can watch on replay any day of the week.

Pokemon The First Movie: MewTwo Strikes Back

This is a bit of an odd one but it's memorable to me as I remember seeing it
in theatres as a kid and it was one of the first movies to ever make me cry.
I grew up on the game and the show, and could probably recite alot of lines from this
so I had to include it on the list.
